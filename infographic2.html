Inkjet Printing

The computer sends electronic signals to the printers control board.

The signals are then interpreted and sent to the printers ink and toner cartages. Then the file is printed by having the inkhead moves left and right and place dots of heated ink on a piece of paper moving down.


Uses:

Printing photos or text documents. Best used for personal projects.
